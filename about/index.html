About

Halide Compression was founded to address a core need that a vast quantity of Web companies face: Delivering high-quality images at scale is hard, and new compression tech should make it easier, but often doesn't. We develop solutions to this problem.

Our Goal

In short, we want to be your one-stop shop for image compression consulting and services. Companies like us already exist for video; video compression is expensive, and has garnered a rich ecosystem around its needs. There are a significant number of integrators who will help video businesses scale, offering cloud encoding solutions, high-quality licensed encoders, and more. Images often don't see the same love.

At Halide Compression, our aim is to provide industry-leading products and expert consulting services to help companies where images matter. We want to play a significant role in powering a modern image compression ecosystem in order to help us all collectively move away from legacy standards. Newer image standards paired with top-tier implementations provide a faster, lighter, and more beautiful Web.
